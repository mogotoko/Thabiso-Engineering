Our Services

Telecommunication

End-to-end telecommunications solutions that ensure reliable, high-speed, and secure connectivity.

  • FBB Microwave & RF Solutions – Planning, installation, EBU, maintenance.
  • Antenna services – Installation, alignment, maintenance.
  • Fiber & Transmission – Splicing, testing, FTTH & FTTB solutions.
  • Core & Access Networks – Integration, optimization, switching, routing.
  • Data Center & Server Rooms – Setup, cabling, power backup, IP phones.

Engineering

Precision engineering solutions to support manufacturing, machining, and casting needs.

  • Machining and casting.
  • Equipment fabrication & assembly.
  • Custom engineering projects.
